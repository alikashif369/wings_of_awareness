Conservation Efforts

Learn more about our projects aimed at protecting migratory birds and their habitats.

Community Education

Engaging locals through workshops and educational programs. We believe that empowering communities with knowledge about bird conservation and environmental stewardship is crucial for the long-term success of our efforts. Our workshops cover topics such as the importance of migratory birds, habitat preservation, and sustainable practices that can be adopted in daily life.

Wetland Mapping

Mapping Ramsar wetland sites in Pakistan to aid in conservation planning. Our team uses advanced GIS technology to accurately map these critical habitats. This data is essential for creating effective conservation strategies, monitoring changes in wetland conditions, and advocating for the protection of these areas.

Artificial Nesting Sites

Installing nesting boxes to support bird populations. Many bird species face a shortage of natural nesting sites due to habitat loss. By providing artificial nesting sites, we help increase breeding success rates and support the growth of bird populations in the areas we serve.

Citizen Science Initiatives

Involving the community in bird monitoring, data collection, and afforestation. Citizen science projects provide valuable data for our research and conservation efforts while fostering a sense of involvement and ownership among participants. By including afforestation initiatives, we also work to restore and enhance bird habitats.
