    The Crucial Role of Wetlands for Migratory Birds

    Wetlands are essential for the survival of many migratory bird species. These diverse ecosystems offer critical stopover sites during long migrations, providing food, rest, and shelter. By supporting a variety of habitats, wetlands ensure that migratory birds have the resources they need to complete their journeys and maintain healthy populations.

    • Offer vital stopover sites for migratory birds
    • Provide essential food and shelter during migration
    • Help maintain the balance of ecosystems by supporting diverse wildlife
